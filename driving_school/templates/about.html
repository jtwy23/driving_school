About

Driving School

Welcome to Driving School. Whether it is your first time learning to drive or you have already had lessons before, joining 828 Driving School will put you even closer to achieving your full UK driving license. Driving School will offer a professional and friendly service which will give you a more relaxed environment while in a lesson. This is what we feel will promote a positive attitude as well as offering a relaxed, positive and encouraging environment to learn. Here at Driving School, we aspire to make each and every lesson unique to every pupil. We also ensure that all pupils are relaxed and comfortable when they are learning to drive with us. Every lesson is well structured and planned in advance to suit your level of driving. Nervous pupils are welcome and we take great pride in helping them overcome their driving fears.

Our Cars

Our cars comes fully equipped with dual controls, air conditioning and many more features. All our cars has every comfort that you will need to learn in a happy and relaxing atmosphere.
